Who is Madras?

Say Hello to Madras! She is 363 grams heavy. One filled balloon holds approx. 4.8 grams of mass. It takes about 80 balloons to raise Madras off the ground. The more balloons you add the higher she gets. At $5 per balloon she generates at least $400 profit. Give her a height goal and she raises more money for the charity of your choice.
