Who is Micheal?

Micheal Callan was born and raised in Ireland. He played rugby since he was young. When moved to the United States 30 yrs ago, he met his wife in the Southern Tier of New York. He worked as an engineer and simultaneously coached the Binghamton University Women's Rugby Football Club. While working as an engineer he has travelled internationally. He founded the Southern Tier Fallen Angels Women's Rugby Team in 2014. Since then he has started youth programs at the YMCA in the Binghamton area. He has also recently started coaching rugby at Endwell High School. Micheal is an adament supporter of women's rights and the environment.

Why Fundraising?

Micheal began the Pick Your Nose fundraising as a humerous way to bring awareness to local charities while fundraising for numerous sports teams and businesses. This past year he has retired from his career as an engineer and has dedicated himself to fundraising. Another project that he has started is called "Raising Madras". He is currently finializing the logistics of this fundraising concept.
